IIT BOMBAY

IIT Bombay was founded in 1958 with support from UNESCO and the Soviet Union. It began operations in Worli, Mumbai, before relocating to its current campus in Powai in 1959. Since then, it has grown to become one of India's top engineering institutions, renowned for its academic excellence and contributions to research and technology development.
