A digital classroom refers to a learning environment that utilizes digital technology and tools to enhance the teaching and learning experience.

Laboratories are designed to provide a safe and controlled space where scientists, researchers, and students can perform various scientific activities.

Librarians manage the collections within libraries and assist patrons with finding relevant materials for their research or personal interests.

An indoor playground is a recreational facility designed for children to engage in physical activities and play in a controlled, safe environment. Indoor playgrounds often feature soft play equipment such as slides, ball pits, climbing structures, and interactive games.

A musical classroom is a learning environment that integrates music and musical activities into the curriculum. It is often found in schools or educational institutions where music education is emphasized.
