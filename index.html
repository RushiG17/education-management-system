INSTITUTIONAL INSIGHTS

Historical Monuments

IIT BOMBAY

IIT Bombay was founded in 1958 with support from UNESCO and the Soviet Union. It began operations in Worli, Mumbai, before relocating to its current campus in Powai in 1959. Since then, it has grown to become one of India's top engineering institutions, renowned for its academic excellence and contributions to research and technology development.

IIT KHARAGPUR

The Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur), established in 1951, is the first IIT in India. It was founded in Hijli Detention Camp, a site with historical significance from the Indian freedom movement. Over the decades, it has grown into a premier institute of higher education, renowned for its engineering and technology programs.

IIT DELHI

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi), established in 1961, is one of India's premier engineering institutions. Originally named College of Engineering & Technology, it gained the IIT status in 1963. Located in Hauz Khas, Delhi, it has a strong reputation for excellence in research, education, and innovation in engineering and technology.

IIT MADRAS

Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras), established in 1959 with assistance from the Government of West Germany, is located in Chennai. Renowned for its scenic campus and excellence in engineering education and research, IIT Madras is a top-ranked institution in India.
